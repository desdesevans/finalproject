Getting To Know The Owners

Family and Friend Creations was founded by Barbara & Destini. Barbara, the mother, has always been creative one of her hobbies over the years is building functional model homes for display. Whenever the family had an event they would always look to Barbara for her creative input, she retired a few years ago and discovered she had a lot more free time to utilize her creative abilities. Destini, the daughter, is a student pursuing her masters degree in User Experience where she focuses on user/consumer focused research. The both of them have been best friends and inseparable since Destini was born and decided that combining their skills, experiences, and knowledge could create something beautiful, and Family and Friend was born.
